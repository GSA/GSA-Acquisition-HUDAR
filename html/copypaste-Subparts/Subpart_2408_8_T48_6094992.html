Subpart 2408.8 - Acquisition of Printing and Related Supplies

Parent topic: PART 2408 - REQUIRED SOURCES OF SUPPLIES AND SERVICES

2408.802-70 Contract clause.

The Contracting Officer shall insert the clause at 2452.208-71, Reproduction of Reports, in solicitations and contracts where the contractor is required to produce, as an end product, publications or other written materials.
