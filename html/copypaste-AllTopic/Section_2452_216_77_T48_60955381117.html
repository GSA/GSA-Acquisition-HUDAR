2452.216-77 Estimated quantities - requirements contract.

As prescribed in 2416.506-70(c), insert the following provision:

Estimated Quantities - Requirements Contract (FEB 2006)

In accordance with FAR 52.216-21(a), the government provides the following estimates:

The estimated quantity or amount of supplies or services the government may order during the ordering period of this contract is ______________________ [insert description of item(s) or unit(s) and the estimated number of units or the dollar value].

The maximum quantity or amount of supplies or services the government may order during the ordering period of this contract is ______________________ [insert description of item(s) or unit(s) and the estimated number of units or the dollar value].

(End of provision)
